Happy New Year!

Sari and Dave are excited to announce our new theater company, Slightly Altered States, and want to invite you to our debut performance of The Thing About Dan. Dan runs this February at the iRT, complete with hot mulled cider and a live band.
 
 
‘Paul and Zip are waiting for their Dan, the friend who keeps their lives running smoothly. The thing is, he’s sleeping with their wives.’


written/directed by Sari Caine Glickstein, produced by David Rigo, and starring the fabulous Louis Kornfeld & Michael Hurst.
 
 
Slightly Altered States is a non-for-profit theater company, made up of actors, writers, and improvisers. We are devoted to darkly funny, slightly-absurd material that deals truthfully with the extraordinary in everyday circumstances.
 
Tickets and other information can be found at www.slightlyalteredstates.com
 
Hope you will be there with us to celebrate our first show, and the New Year!

Sari and Dave 
Slightly Altered States Theater Company
